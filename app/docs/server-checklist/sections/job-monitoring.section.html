Step 2: Job Monitoring Ensure schedulers and queues are active.

All background jobs are managed and triggered Resque workers and schedulers. Resque provides a Monitoring User Interface to monitor workers, active jobs and failed jobs. We can also retry or manually kick off jobs from the Monitoring User Interface. Notifications can be setup but are currently turned off.

Review the below Resque monitoring interfaces and ensure all workers are active. Review all failed jobs and take any neccessary actions. Clear failed jobs that have been addressed or are unimportant. Log-on to servers and ensure that the 'scheduler' processes are alive. 'ps -Aef | grep scheduler'
