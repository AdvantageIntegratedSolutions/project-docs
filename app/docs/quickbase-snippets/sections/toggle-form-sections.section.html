toggle-form-sections Automatically expand & collapse QuickBase form sections with a native button.
  1. Create a new 'Formula - Url' button, set 'Link Text' to 'Expand/Collapse Sections' and add to top of form.
  2. Copy this snippet into the formula field.
If you want your button to have a different title then "Expand/Collapse Sections", copy the snippet to a QuickBase page and update the first line of code in the snippet to match your new button title.
